General Interface Solution (GIS) Holding Limited  公鑒：  
    查核意見 
    General Interface Solution (GIS) Holding Limited及其子公司（以下簡稱「GIS 
集團」）民國108年及107年12月31日之合併資產負債表，暨民國108年及107年1月1日至1 
2月31日之合併綜合損益表、合併權益變動表、合併現金流量表，以及合併財務報表附註 
(包括重大會計政策彙總)，業經本會計師查核竣事。 
    依本會計師之意見，上開合併財務報表在所有重大方面係依照「證券發行人財務報 
告編製準則」暨金融監督管理委員會認可之國際財務報導準則、國際會計準則、解釋及 
解釋公告編製，足以允當表達GIS集團民國108年及107年12月31日之合併財務狀況，暨民 
國108年及107年1月1日至12月31日之合併財務績效及合併現金流量。 
    查核意見之基礎 
    本會計師民國108年度係依照「會計師查核簽證財務報表規則」、「金融監督管理委員
會民國109年2月25日金管證審字第1090360805號函」及中華民國一般公認審計準則執行查
核工作；民國107年度係依照「會計師查核簽證財務報表規則」及中華民國一般公認審計準
則執行查核工作。本會計師於該等準則下之責任將於會計師查核合併財務報表之責任段進一 
步說明。本會計師所隸屬事務所受獨立性規範之人員已依中華民國會計師職業道德規範 
，與GIS集團保持超然獨立，並履行該規範之其他責任。本會計師相信已取得足夠及適切 
之查核證據，以作為表示查核意見之基礎。 
    關鍵查核事項 
    關鍵查核事項係指依本會計師之專業判斷，對GIS集團民國108年度合併財務報表之 
查核最為重要之事項。該等事項已於查核合併財務報表整體及形成查核意見之過程中予 
以因應，本會計師並不對該等事項單獨表示意見。 
    GIS集團民國108年度合併財務報表之關鍵查核事項如下： 
     
     
    銷貨之收入截止 
    事項說明 
    收入認列會計政策請詳合併財務報告附註四(二十六)。 
    GIS集團之銷貨型態主要分為工廠直接出貨、目的地銷貨收入以及發貨倉銷貨收入三 
類。其中，目的地及發貨倉銷貨收入於送貨至目的地或是銷貨客戶提貨時（產品之控制 
移轉）始認列收入。GIS集團主要依交貨目的地公司之簽收單、發貨倉保管人所提供報表 
或其他資訊，以目的地公司收貨紀錄或是發貨倉之存貨異動情形作為認列收入之依據， 
因此等認列收入流程通常涉及許多人工作業，可能造成收入認列時點不一致之情形。 
    由於GIS集團每日目的地及發貨倉銷貨交易量龐大，且財務報表結束日前後之交易金 
額對財務報表之影響致為重大，因此，本會計師將目的地及發貨倉銷貨之收入截止列為 
查核最為重要事項之一。 
    因應之查核程序 
    本會計師已執行之查核程序如下： 
  1.已評估及測試GIS集團銷貨收入認列之內部控制。 
  2.針對期末截止日前後一定期間之目的地及發貨倉銷貨收入交易已執行截止測試，包 
含核對目的地公司收貨紀錄、發貨倉保管人之佐證文件，以及帳載存貨異動與銷貨成本 
結轉已記錄於適當期間。 
  3.針對發貨倉之庫存數量已執行發函詢證或實地盤點觀察，以及核對帳載庫存數量。 
    備抵存貨評價損失之評估 
    事項說明 
    存貨評價之會計政策，請詳合併財務報告附註四(十一)；存貨評價之會計估計及假 
設之不確定性，請詳合併財務報告附註五；存貨會計項目說明，請詳合併財務報告附註 
六(五)，民國108年12月31日存貨及備抵存貨評價損失餘額各為新台幣12,712,590仟元及 
新台幣629,305仟元。 
     
    GIS集團主要製造並銷售應用於3C電子產品之觸控模組相關產品，該等存貨因科技快 
速變遷，生命週期短且易受市場價格波動，產生存貨跌價損失或過時陳舊之風險較高。G 
IS集團對存貨係以成本與淨變現價值孰低者衡量，其中，GIS集團係以超過特定期間貨齡 
及個別辨認方式決定過時陳舊或毀損之存貨，其淨變現價值係依據存貨去化程度與折價 
幅度之歷史資訊推算而得。上開備抵存貨評價損失主要係過時陳舊或毀損之存貨所致。 
    由於GIS集團存貨金額重大，項目眾多，且過時陳舊或毀損存貨項目之辨認及其淨變 
現價值之決定常涉及管理階層主觀判斷，亦屬查核中須進行判斷之領域，因此本會計師 
對GIS集團之備抵存貨評價損失之評估列為查核最為重要事項之一。 
    因應之查核程序 
    本會計師已執行之查核程序如下： 
  1.比較財務報表期間對備抵存貨評價損失之提列政策係一致採用，且評估其提列政策 
亦屬合理。 
  2.驗證管理階層用以評價之存貨貨齡報表系統邏輯之適當性，以確認超過一定貨齡之 
過時存貨項目已列入該報表。 
  3.評估管理階層所個別辨認之過時陳舊或毀損存貨項目之合理性及相關佐證文件，並 
與觀察存貨盤點所獲得資訊核對。 
  4.就過時陳舊或毀損之存貨項目所評估淨變現價值，與管理階層討論並取得佐證文件 
，並加以計算。

    本會計師查核合併財務報表之目的，係對合併財務報表整體是否存有導因於舞弊或

Revenue cutoff 
Description
Please refer to Note 4(26) to the consolidated financial statements for accounting policy of revenue recognition.
GIS Group has three sale transaction types, including (1) direct shipment from the factory, (2) FOB destination, 
and (3) hub. For FOB destination and hub, revenue is recognized when goods are shipped to destination or picked 
up by customers (when controls of the products are transferred). 
The supporting documents of revenue recognition include receipts from customers (FOB destination), reports or 
other information provided by hub custodians and inventory movement record of hub. As the process 
of revenue recognition contains numerous manual procedures, which would potentially result
 in inaccurate timing of revenue recognition.
Since there are numerous daily revenue from hubs and from FOB destination and the transaction amounts prior 
to and after the balance sheet date are significant to the financial statements, revenue cutoff has been 
identified as a key audit matter.

How our audit addressed the matter
Our key audit procedures performed in respect of the above key audit matter included the following:
1. We evaluated and tested Group’s controls in respect of the revenue recognition.
2. We tested sales transactions that took place shortly before and after the balance sheet date, by verifying 
customers’ receipt notes, supporting documents provided by hub custodian, inventory movement records, 
and costs of goods sold recognized in the correct reporting periods.
3. We confirmed the inventory quantities with hub custodian or performed physical inventory count observation 
and agreed the results to accounting records

Valuation of allowance for inventory valuation losses
Description
Please refer to Note 4(11) to the consolidated financial statements for accounting policy of inventory valuation, 
Note 5 for significant accounting estimates and assumption uncertainty, and Note 6(5) for “inventory”.  
As of December 31, 2019, the balance of inventory and allowance for inventory valuation losses were 
NT$12,712,590 thousand and NT$629,305 thousand, respectively. The Group is primarily engaged in manufacturing and 
sales of touch modules used in 3C electronic products and other related products. Due to the short life cycle of 
electronic products and the fluctuation of market prices, there is a higher risk of inventory losses from 
market value decline or obsolescence. The Group’s inventories are measured at the lower of cost and 
net realisable value. The obsolete inventories or damaged inventories are determined by the inventory 
age and individual identification. The net realisable value is determined based on historical experience of 
inventory usage and sale discount. Allowance for inventory valuation losses mentioned above was primarily caused 
by the obsolete and damaged inventories.Since the amounts of inventories are material, types of inventories 
are various, and the identification of net realisable value for individually obsolete or damaged inventories 
is subject to management’s judgment, valuation of allowance for inventory valuation losses has been identified 
as a key audit matter.

How our audit addressed the matter
Our key audit procedures performed in respect of the above key audit matter included the following:
1. We ensured consistent application of policies in relating to allowance for inventory valuation losses 
and assessed the reasonableness of policies.
2. We verified whether the system logic used in the Group’s inventory aging report is appropriate to 
ensure that those aged inventories are included in the inventory aging report. 
3. We evaluated the reasonableness of inventories individually identified as obsolete or damaged by 
reviewing supporting documents, and compared the obsolete or damaged inventories with the information 
obtained from physical inventory count observation.
4. We discussed net realizable value of inventories that were individually identified as obsolete and 
damaged with the management, obtained supporting documents and reperformed the calculation.

Impairment assessment of machinery and equipment
Description
Refer to Note 4(16) for accounting policy on impairment on non-financial assets - property, plant and 
equipment, and Note 6(8) for details of property, plant and equipment. As of December 31, 2019, the 
cost and accumulated depreciation and impairment of machinery and equipment included as part of
property, plant and equipment were NT$23,958,593 thousand and NT$13,330,795 thousand, respectively.
The Group’s machinery and equipment is mainly used for manufacturing of touch module used in 3C 
electronic products and other related products. As the market demand changes, the risk of asset impairment 
also increases. After taking into consideration how assets are utilised and industrial characteristics, 
the Group estimated the fair value of machinery and equipment based on the replacement costs provided in 
the appraisal report, as well as the recoverable amounts based on fair value less costs to sell to 
assess whether there is any impairment.
Since the above estimates are subject to management’s subjective judgment, the impairment assessment of 
machinery and equipment has been identified as a key audit matter. 

How our audit addressed the matter

3.Assessed the reasonableness of valuation method, assumptions and data used in the appraisal report in estimating 
the recoverable amount.
4.Verified the reasonableness of impairment loss on those assets where there is an indication of impairment.

　 資產與負債區分流動與非流動之標準

　　　　　(2)主要為交易目的而持有者。

　　　　　　　者除外。

　　　　2.負債符合下列條件之一者，分類為流動負債：
　　　　　(1)預期將於正常營業週期中清償者。
　　　　　(2)主要為交易目的而持有者。
　　　　　(3)預期於資產負債表日後十二個月內到期清償者。
　　　　　(4)不能無條件將清償期限遞延至資產負債表日後至少十二個月者。負債之條款，可能
　　　　　　　依交易對方之選擇，以發行權益工具而導致清償者，不影響其分類。

　　　　佐證之資訊(包括前瞻性者)後，對自原始認列後信用風險並未顯著增加者，按12個月預
　　　　期信用損失金額衡量備抵損失；對自原始認列後信用風險已顯著增加者，按存續期間預
　　　　期信用損失金額衡量備抵損失；就不包含重大財務組成部分之應收帳款或合約資產，按
　　　　存續期間預期信用損失金額衡量備抵損失。

　　　　1.收取來自金融資產現金流量之合約權利失效。
　　　　2.移轉收取金融資產現金流量之合約權利，且業已移轉金融資產所有權之幾乎所有風險
　　　　　及報酬。
　　　　3.移轉收取金融資產現金流量之合約權利，惟未保留對金融資產之控制。

　　　　　本包括原料、直接人工、其他直接成本及生產相關之製造費用（按正常產能分攤），

4.本集團與關聯企業間交易所產生之未實現損益業已依其對關聯企業之權益比例銷除

   業之會計政策已作必要之調整，與本集團採用之政策一致。
5.當集團處分關聯企業時，如喪失對該關聯企業之重大影響，對於先前認列於其他綜

  負債之基礎相同，亦即如先前認列為其他綜合損益之利益或損失，於處分相關資產
  或負債時將被重分類為損益，則當喪失對關聯企業之重大影響時，將該利益或損失
  自權益重分類為損益。如仍對該關聯企業有重大影響，僅按比例將先前在其他綜合
